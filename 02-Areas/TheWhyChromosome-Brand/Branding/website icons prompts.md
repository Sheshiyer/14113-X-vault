### **Glyph 1: Cosmic Compass (Directional Clarity)**

"Design a line-drawn glyph inspired by a compass, symbolizing cosmic navigation and clarity. Use a circular base with sharp radial points extending outward, creating the impression of a starburst or compass rose. The center should feature a symmetrical triangular design or an eye-like motif, representing focus and higher guidance. Texture the thin lines with gradients of gold (#FFD700) and violet (#8A2BE2), transitioning smoothly across the geometry. Subtle glowing accents should radiate from the center outward, evoking celestial alignment. Set the glyph against a pure black (#000) background, amplifying the glowing lines and creating a mystical yet structured appearance. Add faint energy particles surrounding the design for a sense of cosmic energy in motion."

---

### **Glyph 2: Sacred Triangle (Energetic Stability)**

"Create a glyph centered around an equilateral triangle, symbolizing energetic stability and balance. Encircle the triangle with multiple concentric rings, creating depth and dimension. Add symmetrical, outward-facing spikes or runic details at key points along the rings, suggesting a protective barrier. Use gradient textures of gold, violet, and cyan along the lines, giving a luminous, layered effect. The background should remain pure black (#000), with faint energy trails or glowing particles emanating from the spikes to enhance the glyph's celestial presence. The design should convey protection, balance, and universal energy flow."
