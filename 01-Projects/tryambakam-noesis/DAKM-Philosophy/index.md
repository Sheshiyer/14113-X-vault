# DAKM Philosophy Project

**DAKM** stands for the four Purusharthas (Goals of Human Existence) reinterpreted for a modern, quantum-integrated reality:
*   **D**harma (Purpose)
*   **A**rtha (Meaning/Value)
*   **K**arma (Action/Causality)
*   **M**oksha (Liberation/Wholeness)

This project explores these concepts through a series of books/outlines that blend ancient wisdom, German Idealism, and futuristic "Nexus" cosmology.
