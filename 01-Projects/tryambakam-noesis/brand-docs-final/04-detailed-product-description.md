Tryambakam Noesis is a practice platform for self-consciousness — a system that trains the capacity to recognize, refine, and consciously author the meaning structures already operating beneath your decisions, relationships, and sense of direction.

It is not a wellness app. Not a meditation tool. Not an operating system for your life. It is a field where sixteen symbolic-computational engines — drawn from Vedic, Western, and biofield traditions — converge through a single governing framework: **Kha-Ba-La**.

- **Kha** (Spirit) — the field that observes. The author-drive.
- **Ba** (Body) — the vehicle for knowing becoming doing. Embodiment.
- **La** (Inertia) — resistance, gravity, friction. The necessary force that gives form to formlessness.

The tagline is the architecture: Self-Consciousness as Technology (Kha). Body as Medium (Ba). Breath as Interface (La).

**Current status**: Selemene API live. Noesis TUI live. Somatic Canticles live. Free access — no signup, no payment, no email list.
