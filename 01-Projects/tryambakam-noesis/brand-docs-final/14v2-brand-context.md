### Three-Line Pitch

Tryambakam Noesis is a practice platform for self-consciousness — sixteen symbolic-computational engines spanning Vedic, Western, and biofield traditions, governed by the Kha-Ba-La framework: Spirit as observer, Body as vehicle, Inertia as sculpting material. It does not deliver answers or optimize your behavior. It trains the capacity to examine the machinery of your own meaning-making and begin writing code at source. The system succeeds only when you no longer need it.

### Design Principles

1. **Bioluminescent**: Light originates from within organic structure, not projected onto it. Dark canvas. Luminous elements emerge.
2. **Architectural**: Clean geometry, ample negative space, no decoration. Every visual element has structural purpose.
