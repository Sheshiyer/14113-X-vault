## Content Guidelines

### Voice Reminders
- **Grounded**: No enthusiasm, no hype, no exclamation marks
- **Direct**: State clearly, do not hedge
- **Sparse**: Fewer words, more weight
- **The Anatomist Who Sees Fractals**: Clinical precision with structural humor
- **PubMed x Alex Grey**: When biology is more potent than mysticism, use biology
- **Never**: Engagement bait, poll theater, meme formats, "like if you agree"

### Vocabulary
- **Use**: Kha, Ba, La, authorship, coherence, integration, inquiry, pattern, compass, engine, self-consciousness, sovereignty, capacity, inhabitation, upstream, The Quine
- **Avoid**: journey, path, healing, manifesting, vibration, authentic self, optimization, hacks, tribe, community, Dual Guardrail Dyad, AI as selling point, WitnessOS

### Visual Standards
- **Canvas**: Void Black (#070B1D)
- **Display type**: Panchang (FontShare), Sacred Gold (#C5A017)
- **Body type**: Satoshi (FontShare), Parchment (#F0EDE3)
- **Code / terminal**: SF Mono, Muted Silver (#8A9BA8)
- **Gradients**: Kha Arc (canvas), Ba Arc (interactive, CTA), La Arc (completion)
- **Principle**: Bioluminescent — light originates from within, not projected onto dark surface
- **No stock imagery**. No decorative illustration. Text and geometry only.

### Engagement Approach
- Reply thoughtfully or not at all
- Never chase engagement metrics
- Silence is acceptable — silence between posts is not neglect, it is signal
- If a reply deserves a thread, write the thread
- If a reply deserves silence, give it silence
