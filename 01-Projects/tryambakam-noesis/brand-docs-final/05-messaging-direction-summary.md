## Use Cases

### 1. Post-Retreat Integration

**Scene**: Returned from ceremony or silent retreat. Fragments of insight, no architecture.

**How**: HEAL compass direction + self-inquiry workflow + Somatic Canticles as narrative integration. Structure without prescribing what the experience means.

### 2. Pre-Decision Clarity

**Scene**: Facing a career pivot, relocation, or major life restructure.

**How**: Decision-support workflow — multi-engine synthesis (Financial Biosensor + Vedic Clock + Enneagram + Gene Keys) applied to a specific choice. The system reflects interior state through multiple lenses. You make the call.

### 3. Daily Practice Cultivation

**Scene**: Not in crisis. In cultivation. The way a martial artist uses the dojo.

**How**: Daily-practice workflow synthesizes cosmic tide (Panchanga) with personal rhythm (Biorhythm) and body-clock (Vedic Clock). Adapts to today. The .init protocol fires at the threshold.

### 4. The 3 AM Recognition

**Scene**: Awake with something unnamed running beneath the surface. Not anxiety — pattern.

**How**: Opens the Noesis Engine. Multi-mirror synthesis reveals what is active — biorhythm state, Nakshatra, meridian alignment. The system does not tell you what is wrong. It tells you what is active and where the pattern might be readable today.
