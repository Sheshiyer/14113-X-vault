# Short-Form Hook Generator: Tryambakam Noesis
## 15-Second Hooks for TikTok / Reels / Shorts

---

## Overview

These hooks are designed for the first 15 seconds of short-form video content. Each hook includes:
- **The spoken line** (under 5 seconds when spoken)
- **Visual direction** (the pattern interrupt)
- **Script continuation** (seconds 5-15)

The voice is The Anatomist Who Sees Fractals — grounded, direct, respectful-challenging. No hype. No engagement bait. No trending audio. The conviction is the hook.

## Production Notes

### Visual Direction (V3)
- **Canvas**: Void Black (`#070B1D`) as default background
- **Accent lighting**: Sacred Gold (`#C5A017`) as single light source when lighting is used
- **Success/coherence states**: Coherence Emerald (`#10B5A7`)
- **Data/flow states**: Flow Indigo (`#0B50FB`)
- **No clinical white. No natural light warmth.** Light originates from within — bioluminescent principle

### Typography (On-Screen Text)
- Display text: Panchang (wide, industrial-panoramic)
- Body overlays: Satoshi (geometric, clean)
- Data/engine output: SF Mono
- Scale: φ = 1.618 (Golden Ratio)

### Filming Style
- Static camera, minimal movement
- Direct eye contact when face shown
- No rapid cuts, no transitions
- Clean backgrounds — Void Black or near-black

### Audio
- Recorded voice, clear and direct
- No background music during hook
- Subtle ambient after hook (if any) — low drone, not melodic
- No hype, no excitement in delivery. The conviction is the energy

### Pacing
- Hook: 0-3 seconds (grab attention)
- Development: 3-12 seconds (build tension)
- Shift: 12-15 seconds (introduce the structural alternative)

### What NOT to Do
- No pointing at text
- No dancing or trending audio
- No "like and follow for more"
- No excessive energy or false urgency
- No engagement bait
- No wellness aesthetic (soft light, crystals, nature)
- No mention of AI as a feature
