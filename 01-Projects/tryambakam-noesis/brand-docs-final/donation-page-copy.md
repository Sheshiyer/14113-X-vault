## Frequently Asked Questions

### "Is there a free trial?"
Everything digital is permanently free. There's no trial — it's just available. You decide if and when to invest.

### "What if I can't afford to donate?"
Then don't. The work is accessible regardless of resources. If Tryambakam Noesis helps you develop capacity, invest when you're able. Or don't — that's sovereignty too.

### "Will my donation give me special access?"
Not to the core work (that's free for everyone). Sustaining and Deep Support tiers include early access to new content and physical product priority. The engines, the trilogy, the protocols — those remain universally accessible.

### "Can I donate anonymously?"
Yes. No obligation to identify yourself.

### "Is this tax-deductible?"
No. This isn't a nonprofit or charity. It's direct support for a practice platform built by a single practitioner.

### "What if Tryambakam Noesis doesn't work for me?"
Then you stop engaging. Free tier remains free permanently. Monthly donations cancel anytime, no questions asked. Physical products shipped are non-refundable (material costs are real).

### "How long will Tryambakam Noesis exist?"
As long as it maintains integrity and serves genuine self-consciousness practice. If it becomes extractive, dependency-creating, or compromised, it should end. Your investment extends that horizon.

### "Is this an AI product?"
AI is infrastructure, not feature. The sixteen engines are computational — Rust and TypeScript — but the point is not the technology. The point is what the technology makes visible about the practitioner. You will never be asked to interact with an AI. You will interact with your own patterns, reflected through symbolic-computational architecture.
