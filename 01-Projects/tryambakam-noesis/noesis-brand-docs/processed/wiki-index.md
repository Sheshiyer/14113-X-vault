## Glossary

**Seeker Simon** - Primary buyer persona (exhausted seeker, 32-48, post-therapy/apps)

**Grassroots Sovereignty Launch** - Custom orchv2 scenario for $0 budget, donation-first model

**Grounded Depth** - Visual aesthetic (substantial, dense, timeless, earned complexity)

**The Anatomist Who Sees Fractals** - Voice persona (grounded, direct, respectful-challenging)

**FREE Tier** - Digital frameworks, consultation, core access (donation-supported)

**PAID Tier** - Physical products, 1:1 mentorship, financial oracle integration

**MDS** - Messaging Direction Summary (master messaging reference)

**OrchV2** - Context-aware agentic orchestrator (meta-skill for campaign planning)

**Ritual Objects** - Physical material anchors (blends, carved objects, crafted items)

**Witness Agents** - Internal guardrails that regulate inquiry depth
