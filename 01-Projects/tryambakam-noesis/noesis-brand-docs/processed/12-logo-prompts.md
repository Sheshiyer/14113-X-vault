## Brand Essence for Logo Translation

**Name**: Tryambakam Noesis (Greek: νόησις — the act of thinking, intellectual perception, insight)

**Core Concepts to Visualize**:
- Self-reference / Recursion (awareness of awareness)
- Authorship (not consumption)
- The well / Source (where meaning emerges)
- Stability without rigidity
- Grammar / Structure (underlying pattern)

**Visual Keywords**:
- Recursive, self-referential
- Grounded, substantial
- Minimal, earned
- Architectural, structural
- Neither mystical nor clinical

**What to Avoid**:
- Infinity symbols (overused)
- Meditation imagery (lotus, seated figures)
- Third eye / Biofield Locus imagery
- Ouroboros (snake eating tail)
- Celtic knots
- Generic "mind" imagery (brain, head profiles)

## Combination Marks

### Symbol + Wordmark Arrangements

1. **Horizontal**: Symbol left, wordmark right, aligned on center axis
2. **Vertical**: Symbol above, wordmark below, center aligned
3. **Symbol Only**: For constrained spaces (favicons, app icons)

### Clear Space Rules
- Minimum clear space = height of the "N" in wordmark on all sides
- Symbol and wordmark maintain fixed relationship in combination marks
