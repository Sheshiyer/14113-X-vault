Everything digital is permanently free. There's no trial—it's just available. You decide if and when to invest.

### "What if I want to invest more than the suggested tiers?"

### "Is this tax-deductible?"
No. This isn't a nonprofit or charity. It's direct support for philosophical practice work.

### "What if Tryambakam Noesis doesn't work for me?"

### "How long will Tryambakam Noesis exist?"
As long as it maintains integrity and serves genuine inquiry. If it becomes extractive, dependency-creating, or compromised, it should end. Your investment extends that horizon.
