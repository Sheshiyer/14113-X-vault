## Key Brand Principles

1. **Sovereignty over Dependency** — Success = user independence
2. **Inhabitation over Transcendence** — Living consciously, not escaping
3. **Questions over Answers** — Generating clarity, not consuming it
4. **Integration over Accumulation** — Coherence, not collection
5. **Difficulty as Feature** — The challenge is the practice

## Brand Summary

**Product**: Tryambakam Noesis — A Living System for Self-Authored Meaning

**Architecture**: Mother fractal with 10 nested sub-fractals (Selemene Engine, Somatic Canticles, The Plumber, Kopina/PHAS-ION, Witness Agents, Sacred Burnables, Essential Oils, Orgonites, Consultations & Mentorships, Financial Biosensor)

**Core Promise**: Train you to author your own meaning without creating dependency

**Voice**: The Anatomist Who Sees Fractals — precise but playful, anatomical but psychedelic

**Visual Mood**: Grounded Depth — Deep Ink (#1A1A2E), Bone (#F5F0E8), Aged Gold (#B8860B)

**Typography**: Cormorant Garamond (display) + Source Sans 3 (body) + JetBrains Mono (code)

**Success Metric**: User outgrows the system
