- **Primary Avatar** may evolve (e.g., Seeker ‚Üí Weaver ‚Üí Signal ‚Üí Alchemist)

Each mutation is **witnessed**, not forced, and supports both consciousness development and treasure hunt progression.

**Mutation Event Indicators:**
- Strong synchronicities matching avatar themes and character development goals
- Breathfield destabilization followed by deep three-brain realignment
- Emergent abilities (communication, protection, system building, healing, etc.)
- Access level progression and Bitcoin cipher component unlocking
