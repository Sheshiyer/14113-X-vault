## üéµ **NEW CHARACTER NAMING SYSTEM**

### **Design Principles:**
1. **Vocal Variety**: Different syllable counts, rhythms, and sound patterns
2. **Easy Distinction**: Names that sound completely different from each other
3. **Memorable**: Simple enough to remember, unique enough to stand out
4. **Cultural Accessibility**: Names that work across different languages
5. **Character Essence**: Names that reflect personality and consciousness type
