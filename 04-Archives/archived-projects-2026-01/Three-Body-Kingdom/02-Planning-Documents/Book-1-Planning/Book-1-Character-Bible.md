## ðŸ”„ **Character Development Troubleshooting**

### **Common Character Problems and Solutions**

#### **Problem: Sarah Feels Too Academic/Dry**
**Solution**: Remember her Type 5 core fear of being overwhelmed
```
Instead of: "The neurological data indicates consciousness anomalies"
Try: "This data is making my brain hurt in three different ways,
and I'm pretty sure that's not supposed to happen"
```

#### **Problem: Marcus Feels Like Generic Villain**
**Solution**: Show his Type 8 vulnerability beneath the power drive
```
Instead of: "I will destroy anyone who opposes me"
Try: "If I don't control this consciousness research, someone else will,
and they might use it to hurt people. I can't let that happen."
```

#### **Problem: Elena Feels Too Wise/Perfect**
**Solution**: Show her Type 2 tendency to focus on others' needs over her own
```
Instead of: "I have all the consciousness answers"
Try: "I've spent so much time helping others awaken that I sometimes
forget I'm still learning too"
```

#### **Problem: Characters Not Demonstrating Three-Body Problem**
**Solution**: Always show multiple brain responses in key scenes
```
Character Template:
1. Situation arises
2. Reptilian brain responds (survival/threat)
3. Limbic brain responds (emotional/relational)
4. Neocortex responds (analytical/conceptual)
5. Witness consciousness observes (or doesn't, depending on development)
6. Character chooses response (consciously or unconsciously)
```
