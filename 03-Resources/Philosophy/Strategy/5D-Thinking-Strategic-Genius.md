### Stupid Thinking (The Trap)
*   **One-dimensional:** Jamming everything into a single perspective.
*   **Reductionistic:** Reducing complex problems to a single domain (e.g., "it's just a marketing problem").
*   **Tribal:** Only trusting your own group; rejecting outsiders.
*   **Non-questioning:** Justifying actions with "that's just how it's done."
*   **The Signal:** Stopping thinking too early once you reach the limits of what you know.

### Genius Thinking (The Objective)
*   **The Definition:** The ability to hold threatening ideas in the realm of possibility with the intention to understand, not just know.
*   **The Goal:** To continue thinking beyond pre-programmed responses.
