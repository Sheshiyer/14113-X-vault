## Overview
A prompt template for generating "Woodblock Print" (Ukiyo-e) style illustrations with authentic textures and color planes.

## The "Woodblock Print" Template
### Base Prompt
> "A woodblock print of a [subject], bold carved lines and flat color planes in [color1] and [color2], visible wood grain texture, Edo-period ukiyo-e influence, slight ink bleed at the edges."
