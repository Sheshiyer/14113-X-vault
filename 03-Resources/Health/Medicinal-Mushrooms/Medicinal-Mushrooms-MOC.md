## ‚ö†Ô∏è Safety & Contraindications

### General Safety
- **Drug Interactions**: Check with healthcare provider
- **Pregnancy/Breastfeeding**: Avoid most medicinal mushrooms
- **Autoimmune Conditions**: Immune-modulating mushrooms may need caution
- **Allergies**: Start with small doses

### Species-Specific Warnings
- **Reishi**: May thin blood (check if on anticoagulants)
- **Cordyceps**: May affect blood sugar (monitor if diabetic)
- **Psilocybin**: Contraindicated with certain psychiatric medications
- **Lion's Mane**: Generally safe, but start low dose

### Sourcing Quality
‚úÖ **Recommended**: Organic, fruiting body extracts  
‚ö†Ô∏è **Caution**: Mycelium on grain (lower active compound concentration)  
‚ùå **Avoid**: Heavy metal contamination risk from wild harvesting in polluted areas
