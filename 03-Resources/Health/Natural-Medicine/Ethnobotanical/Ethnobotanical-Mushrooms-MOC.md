### Critical Safety Information

**Contraindications**:
- ‚ùå **Schizophrenia** or family history
- ‚ùå **Bipolar disorder** (can trigger mania)
- ‚ùå **Heart conditions** (moderate blood pressure increase)
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **SSRIs/MAOIs** (reduced effects or dangerous interactions)

**Safe Use Guidelines**:
1. **Test & Identify**: Never consume unidentified mushrooms
2. **Set & Setting**: Comfortable environment, trusted companions
3. **Dosage**: Start low (0.5-1g dried), increase gradually
4. **Integration**: Process experiences through therapy or journaling
5. **Frequency**: Space experiences weeks/months apart

**Emergency Protocols**:
- **Bad Trip**: Calm environment, reassurance, "this will pass"
- **Psychological Crisis**: Contact trained therapist or hotline
- **Medical Emergency**: Call emergency services if needed
