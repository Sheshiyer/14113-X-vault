## 2) Models (named + short definitions)

- **Quantum-of-Choice Model (Arthur Young)**: identifies a basic physical quantum (action/photon) with a basic “volitional quantum” (a unit of choice).
- **Negation-as-First-Choice Model**: agency begins with the capacity to refuse/stop/negate; “negative space” is not absence but the *first mark of will*.
- **Karma-as-Recurrence Loop**: the “wheel” is a dynamical attractor of repetition; liberation is not moral bookkeeping but insertion of novelty.
- **Novelty Hammer / Axehead Model**: a geometric “3-4-5 / triangle head” metaphor for the tool that breaks cyclic recurrence (choice shattering the wheel).
- **Genesis Autocorrelation / Fold-Up Model**: treat the letter-stream as a bead chain; fold so identical (or alphabet-symmetric) letters align; the folding is constrained to a torus surface → yields a compact generative form.
- **Bagel Theory (Torus Ontology)**: reality/text topology as a donut surface; what matters is the surface connectivity (not the “dough”).
- **Flame-Casting / Shadow Alphabet Model**: one asymmetric 3D form whose 2D projections match letter-forms (Hebrew; related alphabets).
- **Hand-as-Alphabet Interface**: the human hand is the embodied projector; different gestures yield letter silhouettes; alphabet = codified gesture-space.
- **Circle–Line Dialectic**: fundamental complementary pair (spherical/circular expansion vs linear extension); shows up as one/many, particle/wave, prophet/disciples, etc.
- **Dynamic “As Above So Below” Triangle**: a unit-line construction yields an isosceles triangle tied to 3-4-5 proportions and (claimed) links to golden mean; used to map between time/impulse and space/momentum; later lifted into circular/spherical symmetry.

## 3) Proof moves (what counts as evidence in Meru; how they argue)

- **Pattern recognition → then demand constraint**: “finding patterns” is cheap; meaning requires a *rule* that accounts for **all letters / no leftovers** and yields a coherent object.
- **Self-reference as a validity criterion**: Torah “compared only to itself” → autocorrelation / folding the text against itself as an evidentiary method.
- **Compression / economy**: the “right” model is the *most compact and economical* representation; elegance is treated as a truth-signal (almost like physics).
- **Cross-domain invariants**: if one geometry explains (a) letters, (b) hand gestures, (c) circle/line metaphysics, (d) physics dualities, it’s taken as evidence of depth.
- **Topology over literalism**: prefer connectivity/transformations (“dynamic”) over noun-things (“static is dead”).
- **Analogy as bridge, not decoration**: Newton impulse→momentum, Fourier transform, particle/wave are invoked as *structural homologies* to the spiritual claim.
- **Respect-the-vessel argument**: traditions preserved the “sweet water” (text integrity) even if the “science of consciousness” inside isn’t widely accessed; sociological continuity is treated as supporting evidence.
