## Course Structure

### Module 1: Foundation - Pranic Energy & Breathwork

**"Am I Breathing?" Practice**
- Develop baseline awareness of natural breath patterns

- Essential foundation for all advanced breathwork techniques

**Grounding Protocol**
- Begin each session grounded (standing/sitting on earth)
- Hydrate before practice
- Prepare body for full energetic response

### Module 3: Breathwork Technique Mastery

**Core Understanding**: Different breathwork techniques yield different results. Timing and technique selection are crucial.

#### Golden Ratio Breathing
- **Pattern**: Based on 1.618 ratio (e.g., inhale 3, pause 5, exhale 8)
- **Effect**: Aligns mind and body, enhances calm and focus
- **Application**: Foundation technique for energy harmonization

#### Circular Breathing
- **Method**: Continuous breath flow without pauses
- **Purpose**: Maintains consistent energy circulation
- **Benefit**: Builds breath endurance and energy stability

#### Wim Hof Method
- **Focus**: Breath for energy, focus, and physical resilience
- **Technique**: Controlled hyperventilation followed by retention
- **Result**: Increased energy and stress resistance

#### Pranayama Techniques
- **Variety**: Multiple yogic breath practices
- **Customization**: Different techniques for different states
- **Integration**: Traditional wisdom with modern application

### Module 4: Pranic Piloerection Induction

**Vibration-Based Breathwork**
- Visualize prana flowing from head to toes with each inhale
- Release stagnant energy on exhale
- Build awareness through energy sensation cultivation

**Visualization Techniques**

### Module 5: Habit Formation & Long-Term Integration
