## The List

1.  **Determined: A Science of Life without Free Will**
    *   *Author:* Robert M. Sapolsky
    *   *Topic:* Neuroscience, Free Will, Determinism.

2.  **The Dawn of Everything: A New History of Humanity**
    *   *Authors:* David Graeber & David Wengrow
    *   *Topic:* Anthropology, History, Social Structures.

3.  **How to Change Your Mind: What the New Science of Psychedelics Teaches Us About Consciousness, Dying, Addiction, Depression, and Transcendence**
    *   *Author:* Michael Pollan
    *   *Topic:* Psychedelics, Consciousness, Neuroscience.

4.  **The Matter With Things: Our Brains, Our Delusions and the Unmaking of the World** (Volume 1 & 2)
    *   *Author:* Iain McGilchrist
    *   *Topic:* Neuroscience, Philosophy, Hemisphere Hypothesis, Epistemology.
