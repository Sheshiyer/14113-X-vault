# 30 Sacred Herbs for Smudging and Cleansing

## Metadata
*   **Source:** `ilmypsychicjane.com` (Jane Luna)
*   **Topics:** Smudging, Herbs, Cleansing, Rituals, Aromatherapy
*   **Context:** A guide to 30 sacred herbs used for smudging, cleansing, and healing purposes.

## Key Points
*   **Sage (White/Desert/Common):** The most common herb for cleansing, blessing, and healing. Removes negativity. Desert Sage is a good alternative for those who dislike White Sage.
*   **Cedar:** Used for renewal, protection, and grounding. Often used by beginners and in full smudge rituals.
*   **Rosemary:** Soothing, encourages peace, and removes negative energy. Often mixed with Sage.
*   **Lavender:** Promotes rest, peace, relaxation, and happiness.
*   **Palo Santo ("Holy Wood"):** Used for deep healing, energy cleansing, and renewal. Has a citrus-like scent.
*   **Mugwort (Black Sage):** Enhances clarity and intuitive abilities; removes unwanted energy.
*   **Juniper:** (Mentioned in title/video)
*   **Aspen:** Protection and anxiety reduction.
*   **Lemongrass:** Purifying, cleansing, energizing, clarity, and focus.
*   **Cinnamon:** Increases energy, luck, prosperity, motivation, and healing.
*   **Blue Spruce:** Serenity, grace, and nobility.
*   **Eucalyptus:** Protection, health boosting, and energizing.
*   **Bay Leaves:** Mood booster, protection, healing, calming, success, and victory.
*   **Myrrh:** Enlightenment, healing, and grounding.
*   **Peppermint:** Refreshing and soothing.
*   **Pine:** Cleansing, protection, prosperity, and good health.
*   **Catnip:** Therapeutic, love, beauty, and happiness.
*   **Clove:** Drives away hostile forces; psychic enhancer.
*   **Dill:** Protection and luck.
*   **Dandelion:** Divination and spirit calling.
*   **Ginger:** Protection.
*   **Calendula:** Purification and ceremonial uses.
*   **Basil:** Happiness, steadies the mind, and peace.
*   **Chamomile:** Happiness and comfort.
*   **Thyme:** Removes obstacles, mind blocks, negative feelings; memory booster.
*   **Lilac:** Soothing, relaxing, and grounding.
*   **Allspice:** Attracts positive energy, determination, and uplifting.
*   **Amaranth:** Removes intuition blocks, aids healing, and adds comfort.
*   **Frankincense:** Cleansing, reducing stress, and easing tension.
*   **Abalone Shell:** Traditionally used as a flame-resistant bowl for smudging.
