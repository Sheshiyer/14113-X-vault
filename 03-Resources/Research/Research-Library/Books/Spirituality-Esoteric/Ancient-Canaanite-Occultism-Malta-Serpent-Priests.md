## Key Points
*   **Palestine Discovery (2022):** A limestone head of a Canaanite deity (Anat) with a serpent crown was found in Khan Yunis, linking to ancient serpent worship.
*   **Malta Hypogeum Mystery:** Discusses the alleged disappearance of 30 school children and their teachers in the Hal Saflieni Hypogeum in the 1940s.
*   **Serpent Priests:**
    *   **Elongated Skulls:** 7,000 skeletal fragments found in the Hypogeum, some with elongated skulls, theorized to belong to "serpent priests" or a distinct race.
    *   **Vittorio Di Cesare:** Archaeologist who suggested these priests bound their heads to resemble the serpent goddess Astarte/Anat, causing pain but inducing trance states.
    *   **Ms. Lois Jessup:** A British embassy worker who claimed to see giant humanoid beings in the Hypogeum.
*   **Jordan's Black Desert Geoglyphs:**
    *   **Discovery:** Archaeologist Virtudes Pont Sanchez identified geoglyphs in Jordan/Arabia as depictions of human and reptile embryos.
    *   **Theory:** Suggests advanced biological knowledge or extraterrestrial (Anunnaki) genetic experiments merging humans and reptiles.
    *   **Dating:** Approx. 8,500 years old.
*   **Ubaid Lizard Figurines:** Connects the Malta finds to 7,000-year-old lizard-like figurines found in Iraq (Ubaid period).
*   **Modern Mystery (Mike Mansholt):** Mentions the 2016 death of German tourist Mike Mansholt in Malta, found with missing organs, speculatively linked to ancient ritualistic practices.
*   **Anunnaki Connection:** Discusses Zecharia Sitchin's theories of the Anunnaki and genetic manipulation.
