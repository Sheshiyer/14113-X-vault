### 1. Western Origins (The Aryan/Irish Thesis)
*   **Thesis:** The "Hebrews," "Phoenicians," and "Aryans" were originally the same caste of Western sages (Druids/Ibaru) from Ireland and the North Atlantic (Hyperborea).
*   **Migration:** These groups migrated East to Egypt, India, and Persia, bringing civilization, writing, and the "Stellar Cult" with them.
*   **Deception:** Conventional history and the Judeo-Christian tradition are viewed as a "holy art of deception" designed to conceal these Western origins and the ancient goddess/stellar religion.

### 2. The True Meaning of "Hebrew"
*   **Ibaru / Hibernia:** Tsarion argues "Hebrew" derives from *Ibaru* or *Hibernia* (Ireland), meaning "The Sacred Ones" or "Men of the River" (River of Knowledge).
*   **Not a Race:** Originally a title for the learned priestly caste, not a specific ethnic group.
