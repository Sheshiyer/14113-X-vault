# Man Bites Dog: Contrast as the Attractor of Consciousness
**Author:** Stan Tenen (Meru Foundation)
**Date:** 1989, 1996
**Source:** [Original HTML](../../../Unsorted/html/Man%20Bites%20Dog%20%2801j1j9db4b5tssc0y0jrwnyfkd%29.html)

## üìù Executive Summary
In this essay, Stan Tenen explores the concept of **Contrast** (Information) as the fundamental driver of consciousness and self-awareness. He argues that just as the physical sun provides the neg-entropic gradient for biological life, the "Infinite Contrast" between the Singularity of God (**HaShem**) and the Wholeness of God (**Elokim**) provides the gradient for spiritual growth and consciousness.

## üîë Key Arguments

### 1. It from Bit (Information from Contrast)
*   Information is based on contrast.
*   Contrast attracts attention (e.g., "Man Bites Dog" is news; "Dog Bites Man" is not).
*   **Anomaly** is the attractor for consciousness.

### 2. The Sun as Physical Attractor
*   Referencing **Roger Penrose**, Tenen notes that life on Earth relies on the high contrast between the **hot, small sun** and the **cold, dark sky**.
*   This provides the **neg-entropy** (information) needed for self-organization.

### 3. The Divine Attractor (Infinite Contrast)
*   To account for *self-awareness* (beyond mere biological life), Tenen proposes an even higher contrast:
    *   **HaShem (The Name):** The most compact, Exquisitely **Singular** aspect (Infinite Point).
    *   **Elokim (God):** The most expansive, **All-There-Is Whole** aspect (Infinite Space).
*   The relationship between these two is **Infinite Contrast**.
*   This is represented by the Hebrew letter **Bet** (House/Boundary) and the Kabbalistic concept of **Tzim-tzum** (Self-constriction).

### 4. Conclusion
The existence of our personal awareness and conscious will infers the existence of this "One-Whole Lord-God" structure of infinite contrast.
