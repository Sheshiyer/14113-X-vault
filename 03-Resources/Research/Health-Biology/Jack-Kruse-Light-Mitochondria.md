## Key Concepts

### 1. Light Controls Metabolism
*   **The Thesis:** Biology is not just biochemistry; it is quantum physics. Light frequencies (UV, IR) control the metabolic flux in mitochondria.
*   **Food is Light:** Food is just a carrier of electrons and protons (hydrogen) derived from photosynthesis (sunlight).
*   **The Eye & Skin:** These are the primary interfaces for light. The skin is the largest "non-visual photoreceptor" organ.

### 2. The Role of Melanin
*   **Superconductor:** Melanin is capable of absorbing all frequencies of light and dissociating water (splitting water) to create energy (electrons/protons).
*   **Locations:** Found in the skin, eye (RPE), and brain (Substantia Nigra). Loss of melanin in these areas leads to disease (e.g., Parkinson's, Vitiligo).
*   **Autoimmunity:** Kruse argues that T-cells need melanin to function properly, and this melanin production is stimulated by UV light (via the skin-bone marrow axis).

### 3. Nitric Oxide (NO) and UV Light
*   **Mechanism:** UV light on the skin releases Nitric Oxide.
*   **Effects:** NO acts locally to vasodilate (lower blood pressure) and *slow down* ATP production in mitochondria (which is protective during the day to prevent overheating/ROS damage).
*   **Viagra Connection:** Men need Viagra because they don't get sun on their genitals (no NO production -> no vasodilation).

### 4. Blue Light Toxicity
*   **The Hazard:** Artificial blue light (screens, LEDs) without the balancing IR (infrared) from the sun is toxic.
*   **Mechanism:** It destroys melanin, disrupts the circadian clock (SCN), and causes "leaky gut" via the skin-gut axis.
*   **Melasma:** Facial melasma is often caused by blue light from screens, not just the sun.

### 5. Water and DHA
*   **Metabolic Water:** We create water in our mitochondria (Cytochrome C Oxidase). This water is deuterium-depleted and essential for magnetic signaling.
*   **DHA:** Docosahexaenoic acid (omega-3) is a critical component of cell membranes (especially in the eye/brain) that acts as a "quantum wire" for electron tunneling.

## Practical Implications
*   **Sun Exposure:** Essential for health. "Get naked in the sun."
*   **Block Blue Light:** Avoid screens at night, use blue-blocking glasses, remove sunglasses/contacts when outside.
