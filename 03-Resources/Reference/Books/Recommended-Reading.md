The program “Psychic Vampirism” is a vital continuation of my ongoing investigation into the roots of evil. Throughout this program, and in others, you will notice that I frequently refer to the same authors and their great works. There are certain quotes I deliberately feature several times throughout my presentations. Many quotes make sense on several levels and take time to fully appreciate.
