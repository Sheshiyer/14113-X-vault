# Computational Properties of Consciousness & Qualia Research

## Metadata
*   **Source:** Qualia Computing (Andrés Gómez Emilsson, Anders Amelin, Maggie Wassinge)
*   **Topics:** Consciousness, Qualia, Neuroscience, AI Alignment, Psychedelics, Symmetry Theory of Valence (STV), Binding Problem, Topology
*   **Context:** A collection of articles and letters discussing advanced theories of consciousness, the mathematical modeling of phenomenology, and new research paradigms.

## Key Concepts

### 1. The Brain as a Musical Instrument
*   **Neural Annealing:** Brain activity is modeled as oscillatory activity (sound) in a material (metal). Psychedelics and meditation act as "annealing" processes, heating the system to allow self-organization into more consonant states.
*   **Resonance:** Learning and memory are akin to hammering the metal; annealing redistributes synaptic weights for holistic integration.

### 2. Symmetry Theory of Valence (STV)
*   **Common Currency:** Valence (pleasantness/unpleasantness) is the common currency of the nervous system, optimized for efficiency.
*   **Symmetry:** Valence corresponds to the mathematical symmetry of the neural state. High symmetry = high pleasure (consonance); dissonance = pain.

### 3. Cognitive Sovereignty
*   **Principles:**
    1.  **Freedom of Thought:** Cultivating child-like wonder and a "Yes And" approach.
    2.  **Idea Ownership:** Tracking metadata of ideas to incentivize sharing.
    3.  **Information Responsibility:** Wisdom in sharing dangerous information (avoiding info-hazards).

### 4. Solving the Binding Problem
*   **The Boundary Problem:** The real question is how a unified reality develops boundaries, not how parts bind together.
*   **Topological Segmentation:** Proposed solution where holistic field behavior and emergent separate beings coexist through topological segmentation of electromagnetic fields.

### 5. Higher Dimensions in Consciousness
*   **Virtual Dimensions:** "Higher dimensions" in psychedelic states (e.g., DMT) are phenomenological, resulting from the interlocking of multiple degrees of freedom (coupled oscillators) emulating higher-dimensional geometry.
*   **Hyperbolic Geometry:** Visual systems may "glitch" into hyperbolic representations.

### 6. New Research Methods
*   **Generalized Wada Test:** Anesthetizing/altering one hemisphere to study inter-hemispheric consciousness.
*   **Resonance-Based Hedonic Mapping:** Modeling the brain as coupled oscillators.
*   **Phenomenal Puzzles:** Using qualia-based puzzles to prove consciousness to others (solving the Problem of Other Minds).
