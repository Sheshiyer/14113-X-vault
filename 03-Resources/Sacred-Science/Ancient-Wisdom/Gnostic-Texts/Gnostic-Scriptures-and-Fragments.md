# Pistis Sophia: The Books of the Savior

## Metadata
*   **Source:** Askew Codex (British Museum)
*   **Translator:** G. R. S. Mead (1921)
*   **Original Publication:** 1896 (1st Ed), 1921 (2nd Ed)
*   **Topics:** Gnosticism, Pistis Sophia, Askew Codex, Books of the Savior

## Description
The **Askew Codex** (also known as *Piste Sophiea Cotice*) was acquired by the British Museum in 1795. G.R.S. Mead suggests the title "Books of the Savior" is more appropriate. This document refers to the complete translation by G.R.S. Mead.
